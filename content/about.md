# About David

David Bradshaw transitioned to professional golf in 2012 after beginning studies with the British PGA. He trained under Master Leadbetter Instructor Andrew Nicholson before establishing his coaching career.

In 2013, Bradshaw joined Leadbetter Indonesia to support the brand's Jakarta operations. From 2016 to 2018, he served as Academy Director at LGA Bali, coaching amateur player Meva Schmit to victory in the Indonesia National Junior Championship and a runner-up finish at the All-Age National Amateur Championship 2018.

By 2019, Bradshaw had joined LGA Singapore's junior development program leadership. He achieved Senior Leadbetter Coach status in 2022, specializing in competitive junior and adult instruction. His approach emphasizes short game instruction and comprehensive athlete development.
